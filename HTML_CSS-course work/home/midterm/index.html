What do we wish before we die? It would be nice to see the Galapagos Island to learn about evolution.From the islands,traveling to see the Glowworm Caves would be an exciting adventure.Admiring the beauty of nature would not end without exploring the Great Barrier Reef.It would be my three places to visit before I die.





Galapagos Islands(Ecuador)

The galapagos is a melting pot of marine life thanks to its location at the confluence of three ocean currents.Above the crystalline waters.This 19-island archipelago is home to a cornucopia of colorful species ,many endemic,including the pink iguana,blue-footed booby and giant green tortoise,which can live more than 170 years.

















Glowworm Caves(New Zealand)

Thia specific spot in the Waitomo Caves is known as the Glowworm Grotto,a place where glowworms create a starry effect on the ceilings.This species is exclusively found in New Zealand and is around the size of a mosquito

The Great Barrier Reef,Queensland,Australia

The Great Barrier Reef is the largest collection in the world,spanning over 1,400 miles.It's so large that it can be seen from space, and it's among one of the Seven Wonders of the Natural World.It is the place to go diving,offering a one-of-a-kind display of underwater life.
